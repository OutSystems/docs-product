# Extending with ODC

You can extend your existing O11 portfolio with [ODC cloud-native and AI-powered capabilities](https://www.outsystems.com/low-code-platform/developer-cloud/) by building new ODC apps that seamlessly consume trusted resources from your existing O11 apps.

This interoperability approach enables you keep evolving your core apps on O11 while using ODC to deliver new AI-powered agents, digital experiences, or high-performance portals.

For further details on ODC and O11 interoperability use cases, architecture, and configuration, refer to [Extending O11 with ODC](https://www.outsystems.com/tk/redirect?g=892fab92-4f45-413d-809e-0cb453e46039).
